5in5 Prototype 02

WEEK 6 - FRI, FEB 28TH, 2020

Guiding Question:

  • What if our domestic products have their own needs of socializing?
  • What’s our relationship with them? Ownership? Partnership?
  • What if these products are labeled according to on their character and social value rather than efficiency and function?

Concept

For this prototype, I imagine I adopt a rice cooker who is a social butterfly, but I've been eating alone lately and my rice cooker is pretty upset. He refuses to cook (the switch was becoming difficult to be pulled) until I accept the rice cooker’s invitation to his friend which is a pot in another home. And finally, I have dinner at my home with my rice cooker, my rice cooker’s net friend-a pot and the pot’s owner. We have a good time.

Would products with needs and values change our social behaviors?

Are products domesticating human in this way?

What are the criteria for its connection and selection?
